Comic Creators

Stan Lee

Stan Lee was an American comic book writer, editor, and publisher. He co-created numerous iconic characters, including Spider-Man, Iron Man, Hulk, X-Men, and many others. His contributions to the comic industry have left an indelible mark on popular culture.

Jack Kirby

Jack Kirby, often referred to as "The King of Comics," was an American comic book artist, writer, and editor. He co-created many legendary characters alongside Stan Lee, such as Captain America, Thor, and the Fantastic Four. His distinctive art style and imaginative storytelling continue to inspire generations of comic book artists.

Alan Moore

Alan Moore is a British writer known for his groundbreaking works in the comic book industry. He has written critically acclaimed comics, including "Watchmen," "V for Vendetta," and "Swamp Thing." Moore's complex narratives and thought-provoking themes have pushed the boundaries of the medium.

Neil Gaiman

Neil Gaiman is a British author and graphic novelist. His notable comic book works include "The Sandman" series, which revolutionized the medium with its dark and mythological storytelling. Gaiman's rich imagination and literary prowess have earned him numerous awards and a dedicated fan base.
