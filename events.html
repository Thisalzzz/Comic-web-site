Crisis on Infinite Earths

Crisis on Infinite Earths is a landmark comic book crossover event that redefined the DC Comics multiverse. It featured a large cast of heroes and villains from various parallel universes, battling to save the entire multiverse from destruction.

Infinity Gauntlet

Infinity Gauntlet is a Marvel Comics event centered around the all-powerful Infinity Gauntlet and its wielder, Thanos. Thanos obtains all six Infinity Gems and becomes nearly omnipotent, causing chaos and conflict across the universe.

Secret Wars

Secret Wars is a Marvel Comics crossover event that brought together heroes and villains from various Marvel universes onto an artificial planet called Battleworld. The event involved epic battles, power struggles, and unexpected alliances.

Flashpoint

Flashpoint is a DC Comics event that explores an alternate timeline where The Flash alters the course of history, resulting in a world without its iconic heroes. The event follows Barry Allen as he navigates this changed reality and attempts to restore the original timeline.
